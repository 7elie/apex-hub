Our Local Purpose

In Kakuma Refugee Camp, connectivity is a lifeline, but it brings severe digital threats. Forced displacement makes communities highly targeted by financial fraud, phishing, and fake job scams. **The Apex Cyber-Resilience Hub** exists to bridge this gap—providing technical education, proactive threat intelligence, and a secure safety net for digital human rights.
